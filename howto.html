Tutorial

Welcome to Discover & Travel !

Travel from one country to another in the blink of an eye in order to learn more about their culture.

Travel to your country by choosing one of the 6 levels presented.
Each one of them is a maze that contains at least 3 monuments to discover.

Once inside the level, use the keyboard's arrow keys to move your avatar and discover the maze.

In your journey, if you encounter a monument, a button will pop-up to ask if you want to start the quiz

Try to answer the questions correctly to learn more about the country selected.
And don't you worry, if your answers are wrong, you can retry
Only successful people are brave enough to fail in order to prevail!

Once you answered at leaast one question out of 3 correctly in the quiz, you'll be rewarded with a hint leading you to the next monument.

To complete the level, discover all of the 3 monuments by completing their quizzes and then choose another country for another adventure

What are you waiting for ?
Pick your level and start the adventure !!
